    When Bella Swan moves to a small town in Washington, she meets Edward Cullen, a mysterious classmate who reveals himself to be a 108-year-old vampire.

    Edward leaves Bella after an attack that nearly claims her life, and she finds solace in her friendship with Jacob Black, a werewolf.

    After the birth of Renesmee, the Cullens gather other vampire clans as witnesses in their confrontation with the Volturi, who believe Renesmee is an immortal child.
